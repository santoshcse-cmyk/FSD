Steps to create account in facebook.

  1. Go to https://www.facebook.com/reg/?entry_point=login&next=
  2. Enter your Full Name, Date of birth, gender, mobile number and password
  3. Click on Submit button
  4. Verify your account by entering the confirmation code sent to your email or mobile device.
